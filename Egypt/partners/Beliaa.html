Beliaa

About

Beliaa, the name has been inspired from the common Egyptian naming for the car mechanic. Beliaa has been presented to the mobile market early 2013 as a smart mobile service that provide GPS road assistance service in emergency or accidents, and maintenance reservation service with cooperation of a number of reputable authorized automotive service centers in a few seconds.

When launched 2013, Beliaa has been awarded the best mobile application by the world bank, and has been selected of the top 20 mobile application in Mobile Premium Award Spain.

Beliaa provides a non-waiting service and a one-stop car service center on your mobile, and the best thing that it comes with discount.
